Frequently Asked Questions

What is Trendora AI?

Trendora AI is an advanced product research platform that uses AI to help e-commerce entrepreneurs find trending and winning products from various marketplaces and ad platforms.

How does the free plan work?

The free plan allows you to discover 1 product per day with basic feature access. It's designed for beginners looking to explore our platform without any commitment.

Which platforms are supported?

Trendora AI supports insights from Facebook, TikTok, Shopify, Etsy, eBay, Amazon, and more. We continuously add new sources to help you stay ahead.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime via your account dashboard. Your access will continue until the end of the current billing cycle.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 3-day trial on the Pro plan. After this period, refunds are not typically issued. Please review our Refund Policy for details.
