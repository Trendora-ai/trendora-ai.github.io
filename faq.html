Frequently Asked Questions

What is Trendora AI?

Trendora AI is an AI-powered product research tool designed to help eCommerce and dropshipping businesses discover trending products quickly and efficiently.

Is Trendora AI free to use?

Yes, we offer a Free Plan that lets you discover 1 product per day with limited features. You can upgrade anytime to the Pro Plan for full access.

What features do I get with the Pro Plan?

Pro Plan gives you access to unlimited product discovery, advanced filtering, CSV export, AI-generated ad images, and daily product updates.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account dashboard. We offer a 3-day free trial before the first payment.

Can I export products to my store?

Yes, our Pro Plan allows you to export product data to CSV for easy import into your Shopify or WooCommerce store.

How often are new products added?

We add new trending products daily to keep you ahead of the competition.
